I remember vividly while attending a conference on Autism Spectrum Disorder, in Melbourne back in early 2012, a speaker remarked about autistic children, “Mark my words, the future is going to be run by these ‘little professors’ ”
Autism is a very complex and challenging condition. With care, and assistance however, people with autism can open our eyes to a more beautiful way of living. There is love to be shared and much to be learned from people with autism. From the simple but powerful art of compassion that autistic children have towards their carers, to the chaotically meticulous drive that guides them. All they need is the right environment.
Education is our only way towards that.
